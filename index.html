Santi Castillo

Video Game Composer

I'm Santi Castillo, I'm 25 years old, and I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I began my journey in the world of music over 10 years ago, during which I dedicated myself to practicing and studying music. For a over two years now, I've been exclusively composing for video games. During these years, I've worked with various clients from all around the world, completing around 100 projects and composing over 200 songs.

My skills are centered around composing music from scratch, whether it starts from a reference provided by the client or simply from a description of the emotions they want to evoke. I handle all aspects of the project, from composition and recording to production, mixing, and mastering, delivering a product ready to be used.
